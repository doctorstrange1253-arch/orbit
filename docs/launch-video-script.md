# Orbit — Launch Video Production Kit

Target length: **90 seconds**. Format: screen recordings of the live app stitched in CapCut (free, phone or PC), with an AI voiceover generated from the script below. Record everything in dark theme at 1080p; keep the cursor slow and deliberate.

---

## Scene-by-scene storyboard + voiceover script

Each scene lists WHAT TO RECORD and the exact VO line. The VO lines are written to total ~85 seconds at a natural pace.

### Scene 1 — Hook (0:00–0:08)
**Record:** Landing page slow scroll; cut to the dashboard loading with the cosmic background and photons chip visible.
**VO:** "What if learning a new skill didn't cost money — just curiosity? Meet Orbit: the platform where you teach what you know, and learn what you don't."

### Scene 2 — Browse & Match (0:08–0:22)
**Record:** Browse Skills page. Scroll the cards slowly — make sure cards with cosmetics (nebula backgrounds, glowing names) pass by. Type "guitar" in search. Hover a card, click Ratings, close it. Then open Matches and show a reciprocal match card.
**VO:** "Browse a living galaxy of skills — from coding to guitar to languages. Orbit's matching engine finds your perfect swap: someone who teaches what you want, and wants what you teach."

### Scene 3 — Connect & Chat (0:22–0:34)
**Record:** Click Connect on a card, send the swap request. Cut to the Connections page showing the accepted connection with the green online dot. Open chat, send a message, show the typing indicator and read receipts.
**VO:** "Send a swap request, connect, and start talking instantly — real-time chat with read receipts, typing indicators, and your partner's live online status."

### Scene 4 — Video Calls + Whiteboard (0:34–0:50)
**Record:** Press Call on a connection. Show the call connecting, both tiles visible. Open the whiteboard mid-call, draw a few strokes, zoom with pinch (if recording on phone) or scroll-zoom on desktop. Show the drawing appearing live on the other device if you can film both.
**VO:** "Then take the lesson face to face — built-in video calls with a collaborative whiteboard. Sketch, explain, and learn together in real time, on any device — even in the Android app."

### Scene 5 — Gamified Cosmic Economy (0:50–1:08)
**Record:** Dashboard streak badge. Open the Nebula Store, scroll the catalog, buy an item (show the celebration), equip a name glow and avatar frame. Cut to Browse showing YOUR card now wearing the cosmetics. Then the Leaderboard podium and the Observatory with the North Star.
**VO:** "Every session you teach earns Photons — Orbit's currency. Spend them in the Nebula Store on name glows, nameplates, and avatar frames the whole community can see. Climb the leaderboard, keep your streak alive, and rise from Moon to Galaxy tier."

### Scene 6 — Trust & Safety (1:08–1:18)
**Record:** A profile with trust score and star ratings. Open the ratings modal showing reviews. Briefly show the report option in chat.
**VO:** "A trust score backed by real reviews keeps every swap honest, with AI-powered moderation working quietly in the background."

### Scene 7 — Outro / CTA (1:18–1:30)
**Record:** Fast montage — 4–5 one-second clips of the best visuals: cosmetic card, whiteboard drawing, leaderboard podium, call screen. End on the Orbit logo with the tagline, hold 3 seconds.
**VO:** "Skills are meant to be shared. Join Orbit — teach one, learn one, and rise together. Available now on the web and Android."

## Generating the AI voiceover

Paste the full VO text (all 7 lines, in order, as one block) into ONE of these:

1. **ElevenLabs** (elevenlabs.io) — best quality, free tier ~10 min/month. Voice suggestions: "Adam" (deep, confident) or "Rachel" (warm, clear). Settings: Stability 50%, Similarity 75%, Style 10%. Download as MP3.
2. **OpenAI TTS** (platform.openai.com) — voice "onyx" or "nova", model `gpt-4o-mini-tts`, very cheap.
3. **Microsoft Clipchamp** (free, built into Windows 11) — has built-in AI voiceover in the editor itself; easiest all-in-one option since you can edit the video there too.

Tip: generate each scene's line as a SEPARATE clip. That lets you nudge each screen recording to match its line instead of fighting one long audio file.

## Editing checklist (CapCut or Clipchamp)

- 1080p, 30fps export.
- Background music at ~15% volume under the VO: search "ambient space cinematic" in the editor's free library.
- Add a subtle zoom-in (105%) on each clip so static screens feel alive.
- Captions: auto-generate from the VO, style them small and bottom-center (most viewers watch muted).
- Cut on the beat: each scene change should land on a music beat.
- End card: Orbit logo + URL + "Also on Android" for 3 full seconds.

## Recording tips

- Use a **seeded demo account** with cosmetics equipped, an active streak, and a few connections so every screen looks alive.
- Record at 125% browser zoom so UI text is readable on phones.
- For the call/whiteboard scene, use two devices side by side and film the phone over-the-shoulder for one shot — real-device shots build trust.
- Windows: Win+Alt+R records the screen (Game Bar). Phone: built-in screen recorder.
